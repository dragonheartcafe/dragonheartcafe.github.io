Budget Option: SKEB style

I offer skeb-style/ 'surprise me' rigging for 60% of the total cost of a regular commission (40% discount)!

For this type of commission, no WIPs will be provided and no revisions may be made until the final files are delivered. Once you receive your files, the commission is considered complete, but you may book me again to make revisions. Mistake fix revisions are free, but style revisions will be priced appropriately.

The majority of models in my portfolio were skeb rigs with no revisions! You can trust me to rig your model in a way which honours your unique personality.
